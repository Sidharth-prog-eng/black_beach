Why Choose Us

With 50 beautifully appointed rooms, every stay is a celebration of comfort and coastal charm. Choose from our diverse room categories — Standard Non AC, Standard AC, Deluxe AC, Superior, Heritage Suite, Heritage Cottage, Family Suite, and our signature luxury, the Signature Suite — featuring a private balcony or terrace overlooking lush greenery or the endless sea.

At Black Beach Resort Varkala, every moment is crafted for joy. Spend your day basking in the sun, indulging in boat rides, water sports, or a thrilling jungle safari. Explore the nearby temples, cycle through scenic trails, or unwind at our library, gaming room, or fitness centre.

Whether you seek peaceful relaxation or exciting exploration, our activity centre ensures every experience feels extraordinary. More than just a stay — it’s a feeling, a rhythm, a connection to the coast. Discover why Black Beach Resort is one of the most loved beach view resorts in Varkala, where nature, luxury, and Kerala’s warm hospitality embrace you at every sunset.
