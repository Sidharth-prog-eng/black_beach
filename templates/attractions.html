Explore Nearby Attractions

North Cliff

A short distance from the beach, North Cliff offers stunning panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, vibrant local cafés, and boutique shops along the cliffside. It’s the heart of Varkala’s laid-back coastal charm, perfect for sunset walks or a cozy evening by the sea.

Distance: Approximately 1.4 km from Black Beach Resort.

Varkala Beach (Papanasam Beach)

Known for its golden sands and spiritual significance, Papanasam Beach is one of Kerala’s most revered coastal spots. Locals believe a dip in its waters washes away sins, making it both a scenic and spiritual experience. Perfect for swimming and sunset photography.

Distance: Approximately 2 km from Black Beach Resort.

Janardhanaswamy Temple

A centuries-old Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, Janardhanaswamy Temple stands as a symbol of faith and history. Surrounded by palm trees and facing the sea, it’s often called the “Varanasi of the South” for its religious significance and peaceful setting.

Distance: Approximately 2.3 km from Black Beach Resort.

Varkala Aquarium

A must-visit for families and marine life enthusiasts, the Varkala Aquarium features a unique spiral walkway showcasing exotic fish species. It also includes a 3D theatre experience — educational, engaging, and perfect for kids.

Distance: Approximately 1.4 km from Black Beach Resort.

Jatayu Earth Center

Home to the world’s largest bird sculpture, the Jatayu Earth Center combines mythology, adventure, and breathtaking landscapes. Visitors can enjoy cable car rides, rock climbing, and panoramic views of Kerala’s countryside.

Distance: Approximately 27–28 km (around 45–55 minutes by car).

Varkala Tunnel

Step back in time at the Varkala Tunnel, an iconic 19th-century engineering marvel built during the British era. The tunnel was once used for transporting goods via canal and now stands as a fascinating heritage site near Varkala Beach.

Edava Beach & Kappil Backwaters

Discover the rare beauty where sea and backwaters meet at Edava Beach and Kappil Backwaters. Ideal for kayaking, boating, and peaceful walks, this destination offers an unforgettable experience of Kerala’s natural harmony.

Distance: Approximately 4–5 km from Black Beach Resort.
